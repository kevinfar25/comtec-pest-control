## Ideas to Sell Them On (for the call)

### Conversion & Bookings
- Replace the current form with a real booking system (date picker, time slots, confirmation emails) — reduces missed jobs from phone tag
- WhatsApp click-to-chat button — huge in Malta, lower friction than a phone call for many customers
- Service area map showing coverage across Malta & Gozo

### Trust & Credibility
- Customer testimonials / reviews section pulled from Google
- Before/after gallery or short video content
- "Certified & Licensed" badge section with actual credentials displayed
- Case studies for commercial clients (restaurants, hotels, offices)

### Commercial / B2B Angle
- Dedicated landing page for commercial contracts (restaurants, hospitality, food businesses are legally required to have pest control in place)
- Contract/maintenance plan page — recurring revenue pitch for the business
- Downloadable service reports or compliance certificates for commercial clients

### SEO & Local Visibility
- Localised landing pages by town (Valletta, Sliema, St Julian's, etc.) — Malta is small but local SEO still works
- Google Business Profile optimisation
- Blog/FAQ section targeting search queries like "cockroaches in Malta summer" or "how to get rid of ants Malta"

### Operational
- Live chat or AI chatbot (already in this build) connected to a real inbox
- SMS or email follow-up after a job is completed
- Online payment or deposit collection at booking
